# Knowledge Layer

Feed the loop with evidence that can distinguish strong marketing from generic marketing.

## Include

- Past campaign performance: open rate, CTR, conversion rate, revenue by segment
- Winning and losing copy: subject lines, headlines, ads, landing page variants
- Audience research: reviews, interviews, support tickets, community posts, Reddit threads
- Competitor positioning: how others describe themselves and where overlap exists
- Brand voice rules: words, tones, phrases, and patterns that sound on-brand

## How To Use It

- Give critics evidence-backed failure modes, not just style opinions.
- Let judges compare options against prior winners and losers.
- Prefer concrete observations from data over general writing advice.
- Update the knowledge layer after every campaign result so future runs improve.
